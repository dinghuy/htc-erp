# Deep Research Skill

Perform deep research powered by Google Gemini's deep research agent.

## Capabilities
- **Start Research**: Launch comprehensive research on any topic.
- **RAG-grounded**: Research based on local files or uploaded contexts.
- **Report Generation**: Export findings to Markdown, HTML, or PDF.

## Usage
- "Research the latest trends in port automation"
- "Deep research on competitive analysis for ERP systems"
- "Check the status of my research session"
