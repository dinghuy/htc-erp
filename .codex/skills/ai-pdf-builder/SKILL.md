# AI PDF Builder

**YC-style docs in seconds.** AI-powered PDF generator for legal documents, pitch decks, and professional reports.

Generate SAFEs, NDAs, term sheets, whitepapers, and memos from simple prompts.

## Features
- **AI Content Generation** - Generate documents from prompts using Claude.
- **Enhance** - Improve existing content with AI.
- **Summarize** - Generate executive summaries from documents.
- **Professional Layouts** - Pre-configured templates for business and legal docs.

## Usage
- "Generate an NDA for my company"
- "Create a pitch deck outline for my project"
- "Summarize this PDF report"
