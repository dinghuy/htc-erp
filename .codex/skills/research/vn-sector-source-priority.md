# Vietnam Sector Source Priority

Use this reference when the research topic touches Vietnam, logistics, ports, shipping, construction, building materials, industrial production, factories, industrial parks, or infrastructure.

## Priority Order

1. Official / policy / statistics sources
2. Associations and trade media
3. Operators, terminals, and infrastructure owners
4. General Vietnamese press and business media

Do not rely on broad press coverage alone for market sizing, policy interpretation, technical claims, operator capability claims, or project status. Confirm those points against more primary sources first.

## Working Rules

- Use official, operator, or association sources first for facts.
- Use trade media to understand industry framing and recurring pain points.
- Use general press mainly for recent developments, interviews, and cross-checking.
- When sources disagree, prefer the more primary source and record the conflict explicitly.
- If older materials cite `vinamarine.gov.vn`, treat them as legacy references and prefer current public portals such as `vimawa.gov.vn`.
