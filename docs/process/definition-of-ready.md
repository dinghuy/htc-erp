# Definition Of Ready

A task is ready only when:

- The goal is written in plain language.
- The task brief links the relevant canonical doc, Definition of Ready, Definition of Done, and UAT/regression checklist.
- The owning module is identified.
- Inputs, outputs, and side effects are listed.
- Acceptance criteria are explicit.
- Verification commands are known.
- Unknown product decisions are resolved or captured as assumptions.
